Our Mission

Navachetana Livelihoods Private Limited (NLPL) was founded with a vision to empower underserved communities through sustainable microfinance solutions. Our commitment to financial inclusion drives us to reach those who need it most.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and providing financial services that help them grow their businesses, improve their livelihoods, and secure their families' futures.

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Transparent information on how we handle your data

1. Introduction

Welcome to Navachetana Livelihoods Private Limited . We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our microfinance services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you apply for a loan, contact us, or participate in our programs. This information may include:

  • Personal identification information (Name, Date of Birth, Gender)
  • Contact information (Address, Phone Number, Email Address)
  • Identity proof documents (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID)
  • Financial information (Bank Account Details, Income Statements)
  • Employment or business details

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process your loan applications and assess eligibility.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account or loan status.
  • To comply with regulatory requirements (RBI guidelines, KYC norms).
  • To improve our services and customer experience.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our transactions.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Credit Bureau Agencies (CIBIL) as mandated by law.
  • Regulatory authorities and government bodies when required by law.
  • Third-party service providers who assist us in our operations (e.g., payment processors, technology partners), subject to strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. We regularly review our security practices to ensure your data remains safe.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or update your personal information held by us. If you wish to exercise these rights or have any concerns about your data, please contact our Grievance Officer.
